# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** 22.04.2026

Thank you for using Modura.

---

## 1. Subscription Fees

Modura operates on a subscription-based model.

All subscription fees are billed in advance.

---

## 2. Refunds

Payments are generally **non-refundable**.

However, refunds may be issued in limited cases such as:

- Duplicate charges  
- Billing errors  
- Service unavailability caused by us  

All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

---

## 3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time.

- Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period  
- No future charges will occur after cancellation  

---

## 4. Free Trial (If Applicable)

If a free trial is offered:

- You will not be charged until the trial ends  
- You may cancel at any time before billing begins  

---
